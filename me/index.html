Hello friends!
My name is Svetlana. I am 43 years old. And I am fond of knitting on a machine.
Knitting from yarn is an ancient
craft, started in the 11th century.
At first, they knitted only with
knitting needles and crochet.
Later, knitting machines were
invented, which improved over time.
The development reached all the way
to industrial knitting machines.
On which large volumes of the same
things are knitted.
My hobby is a home knitting machine, thanks to which you
can create individual things with different designs.
Another hobby of mine is natural high-quality yarn.
Cashmere, merino, yak, angora, mohair, alpaca, and various mixtures thereof.
  High-quality yarn is very soft and gentle, envelops the body with gentle warmth.
Yes, quality yarn is not cheap. It is worth it. But it's worth it.
Because a sweater made of high-quality natural yarn, with proper care,
will last you more than one season.
Imagine that you can order a sweater in your favorite color,
in your favorite design, and in your size.
  Also, according to your wishes, your initials can be embroidered on the sweater.
A sweater can be colored, monochromatic, striped like a rainbow🙂🙃,
with flowers, polka dots, squares, a sweater with your favorite animal,
and many other things. Fantasy on a home knitting machine can be unlimited.
Although sometimes there are models that are difficult to repeat on a
machine, they must be made only with knitting needles by hand.
Therefore. I invite you to my shop. Into the world of knitted things.
Perhaps you will pick up
something from the
assortment. And maybe you
will be interested in knitting
